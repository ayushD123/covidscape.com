More About CovidScape.com

CovidScape started from a one-room office in Bangalore. We started with a simple idea which was to help improve Indian healthcare by making it accessible and affordable. How did we go about doing this? We devised a website coupled with additional value-added services via which medical establishments can search for their required supplies, order them at the right prices and time, and receive them at their door-steps irrespective of their location.
